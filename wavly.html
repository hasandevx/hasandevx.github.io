About Wavly

Created for Precision & Reliability

Voice Recorder & Editor Wavly was designed and developed by Hasan Rezaee and is published by Sajjad Movahhedi. It empowers users with a high-performance audio engine tailored for Android devices. Whether recording lectures, dictating notes, capturing musical inspiration, or trimming podcasts, Wavly provides a lag-free experience backed by state-of-the-art Flutter architecture.
